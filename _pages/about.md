Greeting! I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in [Interactive Intelligent Systems Lab](https://iis-lab.org) at the University of Tokyo, supervised by [Prof. Koji Yatani](https://iis-lab.org/member/koji-yatani/). I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University under the supervision of the [Prof. Hongsheng Chen](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/hongshengchen) and [Prof. Zhaowei Liu (UCSD)](https://www.zhaowei.us/team.php).

My research focuses on human-AI systems and agentic interaction, with an emphasis on designing, modeling, and evaluating AI systems that collaborate effectively with humans. I am particularly interested in designing multiparty conversational agents, training AI agents to make decisions, and supporting human decision-making with those AI systems in complex, real-world settings.

Outside of research, I enjoy alpine touring skiing, watching films, and walking my dog, which influence how I think about autonomy, judgment, and freedom in the age of AI.

**I am actively seeking opportunities in AI research and engineering roles, where I can contribute to building impactful, real-world AI systems. Please feel free to reach out!!!**
