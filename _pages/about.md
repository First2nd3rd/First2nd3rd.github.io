Greeting! I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in [Interactive Intelligent Systems Lab](https://iis-lab.org) at [the University of Tokyo](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/), supervised by [Prof. Koji Yatani](https://iis-lab.org/member/koji-yatani/). 

My research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), especially in human-AI interaction, mobile sensing, and interactive graphics. Through the lens of comprehending user perception and cognition, I aspire to create innovative interaction techniques that elevate human performance and enrich the experiential journey.
